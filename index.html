What arrives
A letter unlike anything in your inbox

Each letter is printed on premium cream stationery in a handwritten-style font, hand-folded, hand-inserted, and mailed in a small correspondence envelope — addressed to you by name. Inside, a keepsake that belongs to the world of the story.

Letter No. 7 · The Wanderer's Letters
Received: the second Friday of the month
"I found the valley at last — the one the old shepherd described before we parted ways at the crossroads outside Mirrenfall. I wish you could have seen it as I did, just before the light left the sky. The grass was the color of things I don't have words for yet. I pressed something for you. You'll find it folded in with this letter..."
— Yours from somewhere far,
E.
